Overview

Purpose

We want to bring families and friends together so they can enjoy nature and experience the beauty of floating down the river. We want to teach people how to navigate the waters safely and effectively. We want to teach people to respect nature and leave things better than when they started. Most of all, we want people to enjoy themselves.

Audience

We cater to small or large groups alike. Come with a few or come with a lot, we can help you have an enjoyable time.
