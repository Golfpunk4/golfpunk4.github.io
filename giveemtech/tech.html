How to run the Windows Defender virus scanner in 6 easy steps!

Windows Defender is the built in virus scanner that comes with Windows 10. This is a great free scanner that will do most of what the average user needs.
Step 1. Click on the start menu.
Step 2. Click on the settings button.

Step 3. Click on the Update & Security section.

Step 4. Click on Windows Security section.

Step 5. Click on Virus & threat protection.

Step 6. Click Quick scan.

Step 7. Wait for the results.
