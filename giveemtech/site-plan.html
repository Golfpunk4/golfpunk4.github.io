Purpose

"I do tech reviews and tips for people who don't want to do the research or don’t have the time." It will also be people just looking for basic tech tips:

Audience

The target audience will be those who either don’t have the time to do a lot of research into tech products or those who aren’t very tech savvy and need things explained clearly and simply. It might also be a person who is familiar with tech but is just needing help with a particular question. It will be for beginners through intermediates in their tech knowledge.
